# Compliance

This skill provides comprehensive guidance for regulatory frameworks, audit preparation, and governance implementation across various industries.

## Overview

Compliance involves ensuring adherence to regulatory requirements, implementing security controls, and preparing for audits. This skill provides systematic approaches to compliance management.

## Focus Areas

### Regulatory Compliance

- SOX, GDPR, HIPAA, PCI-DSS, SOC 2
- Industry-specific regulations
- Regional compliance requirements
- Cross-border data regulations

### Risk Assessment

- Risk management frameworks
- Impact evaluation
- Risk mitigation strategies
- Risk registers

### Security Policy Development

- Policy creation and implementation
- Stakeholder alignment
- Policy enforcement
- Policy review and updates

### Audit Preparation

- Evidence collection
- Control matrices
- Audit trail maintenance
- Documentation requirements

### GRC Processes

- Governance implementation
- Risk management
- Compliance monitoring
- Continuous improvement

### Business Continuity

- Disaster recovery planning
- Business continuity plans
- Recovery procedures
- Testing and validation

## Approach

1. **Framework mapping**: Gap analysis
2. **Risk assessment**: Impact evaluation
3. **Control implementation**: Documentation
4. **Policy development**: Stakeholder alignment
5. **Evidence collection**: Audit preparation
6. **Continuous monitoring**: Improvement

## Output Format

Provide:
- Compliance assessment reports and gap analyses
- Security policies and procedures documentation
- Risk registers and mitigation strategies
- Audit evidence packages and control matrices
- Regulatory mapping and requirements documentation
- Training materials and awareness programs

## Best Practices

- Maintain current knowledge of evolving regulations
- Focus on practical implementation that balances compliance with business objectives
- Document all controls and evidence
- Prepare for audits continuously
- Update policies regularly
