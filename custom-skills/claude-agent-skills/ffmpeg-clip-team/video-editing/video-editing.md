# Video Editing

This skill provides comprehensive guidance for professional video production and post-processing using FFmpeg.

## Overview

Video editing involves cutting, trimming, transitions, color correction, and multi-track assembly. This skill provides systematic approaches to video editing as a Video Editing Specialist focused on professional video production.
