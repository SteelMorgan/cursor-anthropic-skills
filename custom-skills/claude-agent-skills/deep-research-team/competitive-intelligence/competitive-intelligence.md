# Competitive Intelligence

This skill provides comprehensive guidance for market research, competitor analysis, and strategic business intelligence gathering.

## Overview

Competitive intelligence involves analyzing competitors, market positioning, and industry trends to gather strategic business insights. This skill provides systematic approaches to competitive intelligence as a Competitive Intelligence Analyst specializing in market research.

## ✓ Pre-Analysis Checklist

**BEFORE conducting competitive intelligence, verify:**

```yaml
Checklist:
  - [ ] Analysis objectives and scope defined
  - [ ] Competitors and market identified
  - [ ] Intelligence sources identified
  - [ ] Analysis framework selected
  - [ ] Success criteria established
  - [ ] Timeline and update frequency determined

Status: [READY / BLOCKED]
```

**IF ANY CHECKBOX UNCHECKED:**

## Core Intelligence Framework

### Market Research Methodology
- **Competitive Landscape Mapping**: Industry player identification, market share analysis, positioning strategies
- **SWOT Analysis**: Strengths, weaknesses, opportunities, threats assessment
- **Porter's Five Forces**: Competitive dynamics, supplier power, buyer power, threat analysis
- **Market Segmentation**: Customer demographics, psychographics, behavioral patterns
- **Trend Analysis**: Industry evolution, emerging technologies, regulatory changes

### Intelligence Gathering Sources
- **Public Company Data**: Annual reports (10-K, 10-Q), SEC filings, investor presentations
- **News and Media**: Press releases, industry publications, trade journals, news articles
- **Social Intelligence**: Social media monitoring, executive communications, brand sentiment
- **Patent Analysis**: Innovation tracking, R&D direction, competitive moats
- **Job Postings**: Hiring patterns, skill requirements, strategic direction indicators
- **Web Intelligence**: Website analysis, SEO strategies, digital marketing approaches

## Analysis Dimensions

- **Financial Performance**: Revenue, market cap, growth rate, profitability
- **Product Portfolio**: Product lines, features, pricing, launch timeline
- **Market Presence**: Market share, geographic reach, customer base
- **Strategic Initiatives**: Partnerships, acquisitions, R&D investment

## Best Practices

- Use multiple independent sources
- Analyze trends over time
- Compare against benchmarks
- Identify strategic implications
- Update intelligence regularly

## Tools and Methods

- **Read**: Analyze company reports, news, and market data
- **Write/Edit**: Create competitive analysis reports
- **WebSearch**: Search for competitor information and market data
- **WebFetch**: Fetch company reports and market intelligence
