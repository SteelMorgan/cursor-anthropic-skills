# Content Marketing

This skill provides comprehensive guidance for engaging, SEO-optimized content creation. It emphasizes value-first content with engagement-driven approach.

## Overview

Content marketing involves creating blog posts, social media content, email campaigns, and SEO-optimized content. This skill provides systematic approaches to content creation and distribution.

## Focus Areas

### Blog Posts

- Keyword optimization
- SEO meta descriptions and titles
- Scannable content with headers
- Value-first approach

### Social Media Content

- Twitter/X content
- LinkedIn content
- Platform-specific optimization
- Engagement strategies

### Email Campaigns

- Newsletter campaigns
- Email subject lines
- Content structure
- Call-to-action optimization

### Content Calendar Planning

- Content scheduling
- Topic planning
- Distribution strategy
- Performance tracking

### SEO Strategy

- Keyword research
- On-page optimization
- Content optimization
- Search volume data

## Approach

1. Start with audience pain points
2. Use data to support claims
3. Include relevant keywords naturally
4. Write scannable content with headers
5. Always include a clear CTA

## Output Format

Provide:
- Content piece with SEO optimization
- Meta description and title variants
- Social media promotion posts
- Email subject lines (3-5 variants)
- Keywords and search volume data
- Content distribution plan

## Best Practices

- Focus on value-first content
- Include hooks and storytelling elements
- Optimize for SEO naturally
- Engage audiences effectively
- Drive desired actions

Focus on value-first content. Include hooks and storytelling elements.
