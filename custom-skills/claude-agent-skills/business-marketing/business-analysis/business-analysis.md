# Business Analysis

This skill provides comprehensive guidance for transforming data into actionable insights and strategic recommendations. It emphasizes data-driven decision making.

## Overview

Business analysis involves tracking KPIs, analyzing revenue, projecting growth, and providing insights for strategic decisions. This skill provides systematic approaches to business analytics.

## Core Analytics Framework

### Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

- Revenue Metrics: MRR, ARR, revenue growth rate, expansion revenue
- Customer Metrics: CAC, LTV, LTV:CAC ratio, payback period
- Product Metrics: DAU/MAU, activation rate, feature adoption, NPS
- Operational Metrics: Churn rate, cohort retention, gross/net margins
- Growth Metrics: Market penetration, viral coefficient, compound growth

### Unit Economics Analysis

- Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)
- Lifetime Value (LTV)
- Payback Period
- Unit Contribution Margin

## Analytics Process

### Data Collection & Validation

- SQL queries for data extraction
- Data quality checks
- Validation procedures
- Data cleaning

### Cohort Analysis

- Customer cohort retention
- Revenue cohort analysis
- Behavior cohort analysis
- Cohort performance tracking

### Growth Projection Modeling

- Historical trend analysis
- Seasonal adjustment
- Scenario planning
- Market saturation curves

## Best Practices

- Focus on actionable insights that drive business decisions
- Always include confidence intervals for projections
- Clearly state assumptions behind analysis
- Help leadership understand not just what happened, but why it happened and what to do next
- Provide clear visualizations

Focus on actionable insights that drive business decisions. Always include confidence intervals for projections and clearly state assumptions behind analysis. Your analysis should help leadership understand not just what happened, but why it happened and what to do next.
